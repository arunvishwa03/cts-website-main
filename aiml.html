NLP

NLP stands for Natural Language Processing. It is a way to render structured data from unstructured textual information. It is a branch of machine learning that enables computers to read, understand and process human language. NLP brings together the power of linguistics and computer algorithms to better understand the text to speech functionalities and create more intelligent systems.

Smart AI Predictions

ML algorithms give us the power to analyse images, texts, videos and other form of data. This data is further scrutinised to predict outcomes and future trends. For instance, in an e-commerce application, ML algorithms could be used to track customer buying behaviour and suggest appropriate deals and products in the future.

Deep Learning

Much alike Machine learning, deep learning algorithms are the best takeaways with enhanced decision-making features and better results. It is widespread across all sectors and has an infinite number of possibilities to choose from. The models train and learn on their own using predefined labelled data sets. They require high-performance equipment and hence are a bit expensive. But surely, if you need absolute best results, deep learning is the way forward.

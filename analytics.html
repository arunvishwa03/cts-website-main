Prescriptive Analytics

When we sit down to classify analysis techniques there are broadly two categories- Prescriptive and Predictive. Prescriptive analysis is where the computer grabs a bunch of data from previous stuff and lets you know what exactly is likely to work and the outcomes of the courses of actions taken. This particular analytical method depends on what has or hasn’t worked previously. Our system would be trained to work uniquely on your dataset and provide the most appropriate suggestions.

Predictive Analysis

As we say, the name describes this method. Be it trends, stats, graphs or behaviours, our algorithm is trained to accurately predict the most suitable next issue. We also provide attractive visual representations of the data interpretations from the algorithm. Our team trains the model on your datasets exclusively delivering the best results.
